Box Model

Data Science focuses on extracting actionable insights and patterns from raw data to drive decision-making, while Artificial Intelligence (AI) builds autonomous systems that mimic human cognition to learn, reason, and solve problems. They are interconnected, as AI algorithms rely on data science principles to train and improve over time.
